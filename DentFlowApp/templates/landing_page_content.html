Answers to Your Dental Care Questions

Get the clarity you need to feel confident about your oral health decisions

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers and are happy to assist with claims. If you're unsure about your coverage, our front desk staff can help you check eligibility and guide you through the reimbursement process.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening is safe when performed by a qualified dentist. We use clinically proven products that minimize sensitivity while delivering excellent results.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for a routine checkup and cleaning. However, some patients may need more frequent visits depending on their oral health needs.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely! We welcome patients of all ages, including children. Our team is experienced in pediatric dentistry and creates a comfortable, fun environment for young patients.

What if I have dental anxiety?

We understand dental anxiety is common and offer various comfort options including sedation dentistry, calming music, and a gentle approach to help you feel at ease during your visit.
